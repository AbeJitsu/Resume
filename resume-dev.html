Seven Principles for Making Marriage Work by John Gottman — Research-backed communication patterns that work in any relationship. Taught me to listen for understanding, not just to respond.

Never Split the Difference by Chris Voss — Changed how I handle difficult conversations. Tactical empathy isn't manipulation. It's genuinely trying to understand the other person's perspective.
